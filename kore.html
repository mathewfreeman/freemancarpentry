Privacy Policy

At Kore, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This simple policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information you voluntarily provide to us via our contact forms, including your name, email address, phone number, and project details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to communicate with you regarding your inquiry, provide project estimates, and deliver our services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement standard, reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
